## 8. Tool Recommendations

### For Non-Designers:
1. **Canva** — Templates for Twitter posts
   - Search "Twitter post" templates
   - Customize with brand colors
   - Easy drag-and-drop

2. **Midjourney/DALL-E** — AI-generated images
   - Use prompts above
   - Edit in Canva after

### For Designers:
1. **Figma** — Professional design
   - Create component library
   - Consistent brand system
   - Easy export

   - Mockup screenshots
   - Fine-tune visuals
