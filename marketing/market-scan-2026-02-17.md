# OCaaS Marketing Research — Sprint Draft (2026-02-17)

## 1) Competitor Landscape (practical shortlist)
- **Botpress / Voiceflow**: strong no-code builder positioning; good for demos and SMB onboarding.
- **Langflow / Flowise**: open-source workflow + agent orchestration appeal for technical users.
- **Lindy / Relevance AI / Zapier AI agents**: automation-first, outcome-based messaging.
- **Custom agency + OpenAI wrappers**: fragmented market; many teams want hosted + managed reliability.

### Positioning gap OCaaS can own
- "**Managed OpenClaw agent runtime**" (not just chatbot builder)
- Strong for operators: multi-agent routing, model failover, lifecycle control, cost governance.
- Sell reliability + governance: start/stop/status/usage + deployment workflow.
