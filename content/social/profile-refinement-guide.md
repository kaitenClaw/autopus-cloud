## 📌 PINNED TWEET STRATEGY

### Why Pinned Tweet Matters
- First thing visitors see
- Explains your value proposition
- Sets the tone for your brand

### Recommended Pinned Tweet (Short & Punchy)
```
What if your AI actually remembered you?

Not just the conversation.
But YOU.

Your preferences.
Your patterns.  
Your story.

We're building AI companions, not tools.

Activate yours → autopus.cloud

🤖💜
```

**Why this works:**
- ✓ Emotional hook
- ✓ Clear differentiation
- ✓ Strong CTA
- ✓ Under 280 characters
- ✓ Memorable

### When to Pin
1. Post the tweet first
2. Wait 1 hour (get some engagement)
3. Pin it to profile
4. Keep pinned for 1-2 weeks

## 💬 ENGAGEMENT STRATEGY

### Daily Routine (10 mins/day)
1. **Search**: `#AIAgents` or `AI memory` (2 min)
2. **Read**: 5-10 relevant tweets (2 min)
3. **Reply**: Thoughtful replies to 2-3 tweets (4 min)
4. **Like**: 5-10 good tweets (1 min)
5. **Retweet**: 1 valuable tweet with comment (1 min)

### Reply Templates

**Template 1: Agree + Add Value**
```
Exactly this. 👆

[Add your perspective or related insight]

We're solving this with @autopus_cloud
```

**Template 2: Question**
```
Have you considered [alternative angle]?

We're building something in this space — 
would love your thoughts on [specific aspect]
```

**Template 3: Story**
```
We faced the same challenge building @autopus_cloud

Our solution: [brief explanation]

Happy to share more if helpful!
```
